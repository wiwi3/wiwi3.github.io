Camia and CamiaPro Support

How Can We Help You?

Welcome to the support page for Camia and CamiaPro. We are here to help you with any questions or issues you may encounter while using our apps.

Common Issues

If you are experiencing issues with Camia or CamiaPro, here are a few things you can try:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed from the App Store.
  • Restart the app or your device if the app is not functioning properly.
  • Check your device's settings to ensure the necessary permissions are enabled for the app.

Connecting Devices

For a better connection, it is recommended that both devices connect to the same Wi-Fi network. If you're outdoors, you can share the connection through a mobile hotspot. Additionally, both devices need to have Bluetooth turned on for smooth communication.
